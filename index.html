About Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj

Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj was the founder of the Maratha Empire in India. He was a great warrior, administrator, and military strategist. Forts played a very important role in his military system and governance.

Major Forts of Shivaji Maharaj

  • Raigad Fort – Capital of the Maratha Empire and coronation place of Shivaji Maharaj.
  • Rajgad Fort – First capital of the Maratha Empire and strong strategic fort.
