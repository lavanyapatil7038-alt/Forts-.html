Important Forts of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj

Raigad Fort

Location: Raigad District

Importance: Capital of Swarajya and coronation place.

Shivneri Fort

Location: Pune District

Importance: Birthplace of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj.

Pratapgad Fort

Location: Satara District

Importance: Site of the historic battle with Afzal Khan.

Sinhagad Fort

Location: Pune District

Importance: Famous for the bravery of Tanaji Malusare.

Rajgad Fort

Location: Pune District

Importance: First capital of the Maratha Empire.
